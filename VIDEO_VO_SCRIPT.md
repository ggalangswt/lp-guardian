## 3. Visual "Must-Haves"

### A. The Proof Cockpit UI
- **Canvas**: Ice-white field (#f8fdff) with crisp pale-blue grid lines (#d7ecf8).
- **Beams**: Tall translucent cyan beams for structural depth.
- **Typography**: Bold **Command Green** (#063d32); **JetBrains Mono** for data.

### B. Honesty Labels
- **`VERIFIED`** (Mint): Raw chain data.
- **`COMPUTED`** (Navy): Deterministic math.
- **`LABELED`** (Slate): AI classification.

## 4. Production Notes
